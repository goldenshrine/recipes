Rawa Dosa Recipe



Ingredients

  • 1 cup semolina (rava)
  • 1/4 cup rice flour
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (optional)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine semolina, rice flour, all-purpose flour (if using), and salt.
  2. Add water gradually to form a thin, smooth batter. The batter should be pourable.
  3. Mix in cumin seeds, green chilies, chopped onions, and coriander leaves.
  4. Let the batter rest for 20-30 minutes.
  5. Heat a non-stick pan or tawa over medium heat and grease lightly with oil or ghee.
  6. Pour a ladleful of batter onto the pan, spreading it in a circular motion to form a thin layer.
  7. Drizzle some oil or ghee around the edges of the dosa.
  8. Cook until the edges turn golden brown and the surface is cooked, about 2-3 minutes.
  9. Carefully flip and cook for another minute if you prefer both sides crispy.
  10. Repeat with the remaining batter, greasing the pan as needed.
  11. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.

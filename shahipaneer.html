Shahi Paneer Recipe



Ingredients

  • 250 grams paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 medium tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/4 cup cashew nuts (soaked in warm water)
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a blender, grind the soaked cashew nuts into a smooth paste using a little water.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute until the raw smell disappears.
  5. Add the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
  6. Mix in the cashew paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook for a few minutes.
  7. Add the cream and garam masala, stirring well to combine.
  8. Add the cubed paneer and gently mix to coat with the gravy. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  10. Serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.

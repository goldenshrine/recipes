Dhokla Recipe



Ingredients

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-green chili paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon Eno fruit salt (or baking soda)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Chopped green chilies for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine gram flour, yogurt, ginger-green chili paste, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well to form a smooth batter.
  2. Add water as needed to achieve a pouring consistency.
  3. Grease a steaming dish and pour the batter into it.
  4. Add Eno fruit salt and mix quickly. The batter will start to bubble.
  5. Immediately place the dish in a steamer and steam for about 15-20 minutes.
  6. Check doneness by inserting a toothpick; it should come out clean.
  7. Once done, let it cool for a few minutes and cut into squares.
  8. In a small pan, heat oil and add mustard seeds. Let them splutter, then pour over the dhokla pieces.
  9. Garnish with chopped coriander and green chilies (if using).
  10. Serve with green chutney or sweet chutney.
